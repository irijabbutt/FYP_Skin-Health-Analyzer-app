**OVERVIEW**

Skin health is important to our general well-being, our own self-confidence, and quality of life. With the help of technological advancements and a surge in consumer demand for customized wellness, AI and CV-based skin analysis constitutes the core of what is trending nowadays in digital health and beauty tech. Powerful techniques allow the development of automatic, accurate, and non-invasive diagnostics of skin diseases, which can be accessed from the cameras of smartphones at any moment in time and from any part of the world. The wave of mobile apps utilizing AI to provide personalized skincare advice reflects a larger shift toward smart self-care solutions worldwide, as consumers increasingly value the convenience, accuracy, and early detection of skin issues. This work explores this field of recent interest by leveraging the latest trends in CV and AI to enable users to understand and enhance the health of their skin through a mobile application.

**PROBLEM STATEMENT**

Most people find it difficult to diagnose and handle basic skin problems such as acne, pigmentation, dryness, or wrinkles. Regular visits to the dermatology office are costly and time-consuming and are not accessible to all, especially in remote areas or areas that lack resources. Without professional judgment, self-assessment may lead to misjudgment and inefficient treatment. This project aims to develop a skin analyzer application with an embedded AI function. What makes the challenge important is that it democratizes skin health monitoring and early preventive action towards dependence on a specialist. The primary technical challenge is the development of a high-accuracy, robust, custom diagnostic model on the diverse SCIN dataset and seamless integration with a dynamic, LLM-driven advice system-Gemini via n8n.

**SOLUTION**

The solution will be a cross-platform mobile application developed in Flutter/Dart, allowing users to safely upload a high-quality skin image for instant, on-device custom AI model analysis (Diagnosis). The result of such diagnosis (e.g., 'Mild Acne') is then forwarded to a secure n8n workflow. This workflow would first fetch the diagnosis and then call the Gemini API of Google Cloud AI for context-aware, personalized, non-medical treatment recommendations only. User data/history will be persistently stored through the use of Google Cloud services, specifically Firebase Firestore. The application has an extremely simplified workflow with no image editing options and a Username + PIN login to facilitate ease of use and speed of access.

**PRODUCT EXPECTED**

The end product will be a polished and robust Flutter app for the accurate detection and classification of common skin problems using our custom-trained model. The AI-driven personalized treatment recommendations will be provided via Gemini. It will be useful in various scenarios, as it is highly resistant to skin tones and lighting variances. It will be possible to monitor the health of the skin over time and have suggestions for improvement in maintenance. The main goal is to represent a real and reliable application of AI and CV, enabling accessible and tailored skincare.
